EV Charging Grants for UK Public Sector Organisations 2026

Guide to EV charging grants for UK public sector organisations 2026. WCS, LEVI, NHS and local authority funding for workplace charge points for schools, councils and NHS trusts.

UK public sector organisations can access multiple funding streams for EV charge points in 2026.

Workplace Charging Scheme (WCS)

All public sector bodies qualify for WCS grants of up to £500/socket for up to 40 sockets. Applies to staff car parks, fleet charging and visitor facilities.

LEVI Fund

LEVI provides capital funding to local authorities for public EV charging infrastructure — on-street chargers and public car parks.

NHS specific funding

NHS Trusts can access sustainability funding via NHS England's Green Plan programme, often combined with WCS grants for staff and fleet charging.

Schools and universities

Educational institutions qualify for WCS on staff car parks and may access the Public Sector Decarbonisation Scheme for combined energy efficiency projects.
